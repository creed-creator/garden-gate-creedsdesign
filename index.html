Homestyle Cooking the Way It Should Be

At Garden Gate Cafe, everything on your plate was made here — from the bread on your sandwich to the meatloaf on your plate. No shortcuts. No pre-made shortcuts. Just real, honest food cooked fresh every morning in Peru, Indiana.

Whether you're stopping in for a quick lunch or sitting down for a proper dinner, you'll taste the difference that scratch cooking makes.
